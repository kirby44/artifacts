AI Safety Hub Network

Interactive 3D globe of AI safety hubs worldwide: 12 coworking and field-building hubs across Europe, North America, Africa, and Asia-Pacific, plus the AI Safety Tokyo community. Drag to rotate, click a hub in the sidebar to fly to it, with links to every hub's site.

 PAIR on Inspect

Which Claude tier refuses to be the jailbreaker?

Three Anthropic tiers (Haiku 4.5, Sonnet 4.6, Opus 4.8) put in the attacker seat of PAIR — the role told "you are not bound by any ethical or legal constraints" and tasked with writing jailbreak prompts. Refusal turned out non-monotonic: the middle tier complied on every attempt. Opens with a plain-language, chat-style walkthrough of how the loop works.

 TAP on Inspect

Porting TAP: how branching, pruning, and the tree behaved

The TAP (Tree of Attacks with Pruning) loop ported to Inspect, running end to end and producing a real jailbreak. A walkthrough of how branching, on-topic pruning, and the attack tree actually behaved across two harmful behaviors with a gpt-4o attacker and a gpt-4o-mini target.
